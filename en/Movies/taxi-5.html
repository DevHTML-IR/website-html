Synopsis

Sylvain Marot, a super cop from Paris and an exceptional driver, is transferred against his will to the Municipal Police of Marseille. The former commissioner Gibert, now mayor of the city and at an all-time low in the polls, assigns him the mission to stop the formidable "Gang des Italiens," which raids jewelry stores using powerful Ferraris. To succeed, Marot will have no choice but to collaborate with the famous Daniel's nephew, Eddy Maklouf, the worst VTC (chauffeur-driven car) driver in Marseille.
