Synopsis

We all have a superhero inside us, it just takes a bit of magic to bring it out. For 14-year-old Billy Batson, a streetwise foster kid, all it takes is shouting Shazam! to transform into the adult superhero. Teen in a perfect adult body, Shazam revels in this adult version of himself with the recklessness of a child. However, he'll need to master his powers quickly to fight the dark forces of Dr. Thaddeus Sivana.
